About Earth & Moon

Earth & Moon is a small, eco-friendly, and sustainable business focused on delivering elegant handcrafted jewellery and other assorted products to our customers while avoiding the massive waste and pollution made common by modern consumerism.

Orders are wrapped in recycled paper for protection where possible to cut down on the amount of plastic/bubble wrap needed and are packed in boxes from our own shopping. We strive to recycle everything!

We place great importance on the preservation of Irish wildlife. We donate a portion of profits made from listed products toward rehabilitation of hedgehogs throughout Ireland.
